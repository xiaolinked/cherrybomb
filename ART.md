Hero (Blue Rectangle)
	•	Solid blue fill
	•	Slight outline
	•	Brief white flash when damaged
	•	Stretch slightly when dashing (juice)

Enemy (Yellow Triangle)
	•	Triangle always points toward the hero
	•	Shielded:
	•	Light-blue outline or aura
	•	Armed:
	•	Triangle shakes slightly
	•	Red glow from bomb

Bomb (Red Circle)
	•	Idle: solid red
	•	Armed:
	•	Pulsing scale
	•	Bright glow
	•	Countdown number above it
	•	Explosion:
	•	Expanding red/orange circle
	•	Ring outline for radius clarity
